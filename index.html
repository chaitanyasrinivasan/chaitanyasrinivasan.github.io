Using metabolomics and lipidomics to crack the code on asparaginase-associated pancreatitis (AAP)
Chaitanya Srinivasan*, Li Wen, Amitava Mukherjee, Sohail Z. Husain
Carnegie Mellon University, Meeting of the Minds Symposium, 2018.
UPMC Children's Hospital of Pittsburgh, John G. Rangos Sr. Rangos Research Center Symposium, 2017.
NASAPGHAN Abstract

We used a non-targeted metabolomic and lipidomic platform to determine whether there are unique biomarker signatures that predict the development of asparaginase-associated pancreatitis.
